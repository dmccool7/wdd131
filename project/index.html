Etiquette

  • Don't be loud in public (including trains).
  • Don't eat or drink while you're walking.
  • Bow your head when saying thank you.
  • Take things (like money or train tickets) with two hands. Same with giving.
  • Use chopstick etiquette.
  • There is a standing lane and a walking lane on escalators.
  • Don't sit on the floor or on the curb/sidewalk.
  • Don't tip service workers.
  • Leave phone on silent in public.
  • Don't jaywalk.
  • Don't express affection in public (PDA).
  • There is usually a tray to put your cash or card on when paying, instead of handing it to the worker.
